Nyarugenge District

The historic heart of Kigali — home to Nyamirambo, vibrant markets, cultural sites, and authentic local life.

🏘️ Discover Nyarugenge & Nyamirambo

Nyarugenge is the oldest district in Kigali, featuring the bustling Nyamirambo neighborhood, known for its lively streets, colorful markets, music scene, and rich Swahili-influenced culture. It's the soul of Kigali.

Nyamirambo Streets

Photography and walking tours.

Local Markets

Fresh produce, fabrics, crafts.

Multicultural Heritage

Mosques, art, and fusion cuisine.

⭐ Must-Visit Spots in Nyarugenge

  • Nyamirambo Women's Center - Handmade crafts & tours
  • Green Mosque (Masjid Al-Fatah) - Architectural landmark
  • Kandt House Museum - Natural history museum
  • Nyamirambo Nightlife - Live music & local bars
