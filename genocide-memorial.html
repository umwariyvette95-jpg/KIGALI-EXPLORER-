Kigali Genocide Memorial

Honor the memory of over 250,000 victims and learn about Rwanda's journey of resilience, forgiveness, and hope.

🕊️ A Place of Remembrance & Education

The Kigali Genocide Memorial is the final resting place for more than 250,000 victims of the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi. It serves as a place of remembrance, education, and a testament to Rwanda's strength in rebuilding.

Visiting hours: Daily 8am - 5pm (last entry 4pm). Guided tours available.
